❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common questions about Focusmate and how to make the most of your focus time.

💡 What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that encourages short bursts of focused work (usually 25 minutes) followed by short breaks (typically 5 minutes).

⏱ How do I start the timer?

Click the play button ▶ to begin your session. The timer will automatically switch between focus and break sessions based on your settings.

⚙️ Can I change the focus or break durations?

Yes! Use the + and - buttons under "Focus Session" and "Break Session" to increase or decrease the time before starting your session.

🌙 How do I enable dark mode?

Toggle the switch in the top right corner to activate dark mode. This can help reduce eye strain, especially when working at night.

📬 Is there a way to contact you?

Of course! Head over to our Contact page and send us a message. We’d love to hear your feedback, suggestions, or questions.

🔒 Is my data stored anywhere?

Nope. Focusmate is a simple productivity tool and doesn’t collect or store your session data. It’s all local and private.
